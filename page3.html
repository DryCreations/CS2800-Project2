The Opening Theme

Although Departure! by Masatoshi Ono was used as the opening in all seasons there were actually two versions, the first used in the 1st, 3rd and 5th openings, while the second version was used in openings 2 and 4.

Masatoshi Ono

Masatoshi Ono is a singer songwriter in a japanese rock/heavy metal band. His work has been used in many anime soundtracks in his career including:

  • Dragon Ball Kai
  • Cardfight Vanguard
  • Kishin Houkou Demonbane
  • Mama wa Poyopoyo Saurus ga Osuki
  • Neppuu Kairiku Bushi Road
  • Rainbow: Nisha Rokubou no Shichinin
